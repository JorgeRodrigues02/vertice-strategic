Vértice Strategic Advisory is an independent boutique advisory firm focused on the structuring and intermediation of strategic transactions between entrepreneurs and investors.

We support critical decisions involving investments, partnerships and business exits, with a disciplined approach centred on risk mitigation, capital structure and long-term financial sustainability.

Our role is to bring clarity, alignment and analytical rigour to complex situations — acting independently, without conflicts of interest, to ensure value is properly structured before capital is committed.
